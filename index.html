My Story

Hello there! I'm Aerhone, a multifaceted individual with a passion for continuous learning and diverse experiences. Let me take you on a journey through my professional and personal endeavors.

In 2016, I proudly graduated with a degree in Mechanical Engineering and acquired my engineering license, marking the beginning of my journey in the world of property management. Over the years, I've worn various hats in this field, taking on roles such as Building Engineer, Project Engineer, Procurement Engineer, and ultimately evolving into a Property Manager. The challenges and intricacies of managing properties have honed my problem-solving skills and instilled in me a deep appreciation for effective teamwork.

Despite finding success in this field, my passion for technology and the ever-evolving world of web development beckoned. By year 2023, in pursuit of this passion, I took a leap of faith, quitting my job to immerse myself in the world of coding and development. It's been a thrilling ride, and I'm excited about the endless possibilities that lie ahead.

On days when I'm not immersed in the digital realm, you can find me contributing to my fiancée's business. Collaborating on her ventures not only fuels my entrepreneurial spirit but also allows me to bring a unique perspective to the table.

Beyond the professional realm, I find joy in a variety of interests. Video games are my digital playground, offering an escape into fantastical worlds and a chance to challenge my strategic thinking. When I'm not navigating virtual realms, you might catch me on the basketball court, reveling in the fast-paced excitement of the game. Additionally, I channel my creativity through drawing, finding solace and inspiration in the strokes of a pen or the hues of a digital canvas.
